### What Was Researched

Eight iOS design patterns specifically tailored for **WakeTask** (task/accountability alarm app for US iPhone users with chronic oversleeping):

1. **Multi-Stage Alarm Dismissal** – Puzzle/scan/confirm gates prevent snooze reflex
2. **Full-Screen Alarm Presentation** – Apple HIG-compliant immersive UI that breaks through
3. **Task Accountability Flow** – WakeTask's differentiator: showing *why* users wake, not just puzzles
4. **Barcode/QR Verification** – Location-based friction forcing movement
5. **Snooze Decay Mechanics** – Progressive snooze restrictions + escalation
6. **Deep Link Notification Flow** – Notification-to-app routing via URL schemes
7. **Accessibility (Haptics + VoiceOver)** – Inclusive wake-up experience
8. **Morning Routine Chain** – Post-alarm checklist to anchor routine
