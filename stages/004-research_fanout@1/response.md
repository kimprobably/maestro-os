### 📦 Deliverables

**6 iOS Design Patterns** identified for WakeTask (wake-up alarm product):

1. **Motion-Lock UX** — Shake detection (Alarmy pattern) → prevents accidental dismissal
2. **Progressive Friction** — Adaptive task difficulty based on snooze history
3. **Temporal Gates** — 30-60 sec ramp + task unlock gates (Rise + Alarmy synthesis)
4. **Haptic & Sensory Overload** — Configurable profiles (Gentle/Harsh/ADHD modes)
5. **Social Accountability** — Privacy-first accountability partner feature (CloudKit)
6. **Task Modalities** — Multi-modal tasks (shake, puzzle, movement, photo, QR, voice)

### 🎯 MVP Priority

Phase 1: Temporal gates + heavy haptics + multi-modal tasks (shake + puzzle) + accessibility  
Phase 2: Progressive friction tracking + sensory profile UI + partner widget  
Phase 3: Task variety + social leaderboard + predictive analytics
