# Outreach Voice Review

Review `.maestro/outreach/variants.json` against `knowledge/voice.md`.

Write `.maestro/outreach/voice-review.md`.

Score each variant on:

- specificity;
- offer clarity;
- voice match;
- CTA clarity;
- brevity;
- differentiation.

Mark variants as `pass` or `fail`. At least one variant must pass for approval.

End with exactly one verdict line:

`VERDICT: APPROVED`

or

`VERDICT: REJECTED`
