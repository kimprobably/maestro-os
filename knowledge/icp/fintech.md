# Fintech ICP

Status: fixture for outreach evals

## Target Accounts

- B2B fintech or vertical SaaS teams selling to finance, insurance, or payments.
- Post-revenue teams with visible GTM motion.
- Companies where better list building, enrichment, or outbound workflow quality
  can materially change pipeline.

## Good Signals

- Recent funding, launch, hiring, or expansion.
- Clear sales-led or founder-led outbound motion.
- Technical product with a narrow buyer persona.
- Evidence of manual research, enrichment, or segmentation bottlenecks.

## Avoid

- Consumer-only financial products.
- Companies with no visible B2B sales motion.
- Agencies selling the same core GTM automation service.
- Teams that would need broad education before caring about the offer.

## Offer Angle

Show how to wire a repeatable GTM workflow that turns account signals into
qualified lists, useful enrichment fields, and short operator-grade outbound.
